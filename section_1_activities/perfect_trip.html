The perfect trip

Yucatan, also known as the hidden gem of Mexico, has some of the most beautiful scenery the world. It is green and sunny year-round. It has a rich cultural heritage and is a place that all people interested in ancient history and archeology must visit.

Yucatan is one of my favorite places on earth. I have been there on a study abroad and I love the food more than any other place I have been. It is also the homeland of my wife, which makes me love it even more. Yucatan has a lot of Mayan influence and the mayan culture is still very much alive there. It is an amazing place.
